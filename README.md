**Carbon ROI Intelligence**

A personalized sustainability intelligence platform that helps individuals understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through practical actions and personalized insights.

Rather than providing generic environmental advice, Carbon ROI Intelligence analyzes a user’s lifestyle, identifies their highest-impact emission sources, and recommends realistic actions based on feasibility, expected impact, and personal constraints.

**Many existing carbon calculators provide numerical estimates but fail to explain:**

* Why emissions occur
* Which actions matter most
* Which recommendations are actually realistic
* How individual constraints affect sustainability decisions

**Carbon ROI Intelligence addresses this gap by combining carbon footprint estimation with personalized recommendation ranking and contextual explanations.**

**Key Features**

**Carbon Footprint Analysis**

Estimate monthly carbon emissions across:

* Transportation
* Household Energy
* Food Consumption

**Personalized Recommendations**

Generate ranked opportunities based on:

* Expected carbon savings
* Recommendation confidence
* Lifestyle compatibility
* Practical feasibility

**Carbon Blueprint**

Identify the highest-impact action for a user and explain:

* Why it was selected
* Potential monthly savings
* Expected environmental impact

**Advanced Lifestyle Analysis**

Optional advanced assessment provides deeper insights into:

* Waste & Recycling Habits
* Shopping & Consumer Goods
* Food Sourcing Preferences
* Renewable Energy Adoption
* Sustainability Practices

**Dynamic Opportunity Ranking**

Recommendations are prioritized using a Carbon ROI framework that balances:

* Impact
* Feasibility
* Confidence
* Personal Fit

**Local Profile Management**

No authentication required.

User profiles are stored locally and automatically loaded on future visits using browser storage.

Export Sustainability Report

**Generate a personalized sustainability report containing:**

* Carbon footprint summary
* Emission breakdown
* Blueprint recommendation
* Ranked opportunities
* Personalized explanations
* Advanced analysis findings (if available)

**How It Works**

Step 1

Step 3

Step 4

**Design Philosophy**

Carbon ROI Intelligence is built around a simple idea:

People do not reduce emissions because they are told to.

People reduce emissions when recommendations are realistic, understandable, and aligned with their lifestyle.

The platform focuses on practical sustainability rather than guilt-driven environmental scoring.
